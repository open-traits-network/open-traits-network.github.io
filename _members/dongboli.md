Dongbo is an experimental ecologist based in United Kingdom.

## Collaboration statement
I am interested in collaborative research that explores how environmental change and landscape structure influence ecological dynamics and traits. My work focuses on linking traits to population dynamics and species interactions, particularly under climate warming, environmental variability, and extreme events.

I am happy to participate in collaborative projects, working groups, and community activities within the network, and I welcome opportunities to connect with researchers working on trait-based approaches in ecology and environmental change.
